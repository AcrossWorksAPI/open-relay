# Code Of Conduct

Open Relay is a small open-source project for people working with AI-assisted
software workflows. We want discussion to be direct, useful, and humane.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient with people who are learning.
- Critique ideas, code, and tradeoffs without attacking people.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions when context is missing.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the work.
- Credit sources and contributors when using their ideas or examples.
- Protect privacy: do not post secrets, private logs, private prompts, or
  sensitive repository context.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues, reviews, or discussions.
- Bad-faith security disclosures or exploit details in public threads.
- Spam, impersonation, or other behavior that makes the project unsafe to use.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests;
block users; or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its
contributors.

If you see a concern, contact a maintainer through GitHub. For security issues,
use the process in `SECURITY.md`.
